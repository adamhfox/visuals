The Visual Network Solutions Technology Group Are Experts At Migration

The time for migration comes for every business or operation. Data migration is shifting your services from one application or program to another. These transitions are performed for a variety of reasons. The most common is due to advancement in technology or required upgrade. Technology is advancing every day, and we are always gaining new capabilities. This makes data migration part of your evolution as a business. It is a process that will be routinely required as you outgrow the old tools and new ones become available.

Visual Network Solutions has become the experts in performing data migration. We have moved thousands of user mailboxes to the Office 365 application. Microsoft’s Office 365 is a subscription service that is part of their overall Microsoft Office product line. This bundle enables you to have access to the complete Microsoft Office software suite of products throughout the life cycle of your subscription terms. Beyond migrating mailboxes, the migration to Office 365 includes a variety of products that will be at your disposal:

  • Outlook email service
  • Hosted services like Skype for Business, Sharepoint, and Exchange Online
  • Office desktop applications like Word, Excel, Powerpoint, and Publisher
  • Collaboration tools like OneDrive for Business, SharePoint Online, and Outlook Online

Another bonus of the subscription service is the routine updates, as a subscriber, you are entitled to new versions of software that become available and no longer have to pay the upfront purchase price.

Data Migration Is All About A Seamless Transition

Data migration can be a complex process, but the services we offer do make the procedure more straightforward. Cloud migrations are a streamlined strategy and applications like Office 365 make things go quicker. The basics of the process start with us creating a map of your current data and overlaying that onto the new application. This allows us to make sure that all of your data is properly re-formatted and will function correctly in the new program. Once things are mapped out, we begin the transition. Our experts will monitor the entire process to double-check everything is running smoothly. Our efforts will make your migration process seamless and without disrupting your daily operations. When you get down to the nuts and bolts of data migration, there are multiple ways to conduct the process.

  • Cutover migration
  • Staged migration
  • Hybrid migration

Office 365 hybrid migration is used to maintain on-site and online mailboxes simultaneously while we gradually transition your services to the Office 365 application. Visual Network Solutions’s mission has always been about solving your problems. Data migrations to services like Office 365 are one of the ways we solve future issues before they happen and protect your operations for the long term.
